Our Objectives

Healthcare & Medical Relief

To promote healthcare and medical relief by providing financial assistance and support to persons of all age groups for medical treatment, hospitalization, medicines, surgeries, diagnostic services and other essential healthcare needs; and to organize and conduct health awareness programs, preventive health check-ups, vaccination drives, medical camps and similar initiatives aimed at improving public health and ensuring access to quality and affordable healthcare services for underprivileged and economically weaker sections of society.

Education Support

To advance education by supporting school-going children and students, particularly those from economically weaker sections, by providing stationery, books, uniforms, study materials, digital learning resources, and other educational aids. The Foundation may also grant scholarships, pay tuition fees, examination fees, and provide financial assistance to ensure inclusive access to quality education and promote academic excellence and overall development.

Livelihood Development

To promote livelihood, skill development, and self-reliance by providing financial support, training, guidance, and resources to underprivileged individuals for setting up small businesses, micro-enterprises, self-employment ventures, or income-generating activities. The Foundation may conduct vocational training programs, entrepreneurship development initiatives, and capacity-building workshops to enable sustainable economic independence and improved standards of living.

Social Welfare

To undertake charitable activities for the welfare, relief, and upliftment of underprivileged, marginalized, and economically weaker communities, including but not limited to initiatives in healthcare, education, livelihood development, social welfare, community development, and humanitarian assistance, with the objective of promoting social justice, equality, and inclusive growth.

Partnership & Collaboration

To collaborate, associate, or enter into partnerships with institutions, charitable organizations, non-governmental organizations, corporate bodies, and government authorities at local, national, and international levels for implementing schemes, programs, and projects that further the objectives of healthcare, education, livelihood promotion, and social welfare in a lawful and organized manner.
