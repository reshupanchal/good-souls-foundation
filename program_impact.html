Welcome to S.H.A.M.A. Programs

Support, Health, Advancement and Mentorship Association creating sustainable transformation through healthcare, education and empowerment initiatives.

Support (S)

Building a comprehensive ecosystem that lifts families from vulnerability to lasting self-reliance.

Health (H)

Eliminating financial barriers to critical pediatric healthcare and essential nutrition.

Advancement (A)

Supporting students through tuition assistance, educational kits and academic opportunities.

Mentorship (M)

Empowering youth and women through skill development, guidance and entrepreneurship support.

Healthcare Initiative

We contribute towards easing the healthcare burdens of underprivileged families.

No family should have to stand alone when a child's health is at stake. Through our Healthcare initiative, we contribute towards dismantling the financial and physical barriers that block children from economically weaker sections from receiving timely care.

Our Focus Areas
  • Surgical & Treatment Relief: Life-saving pediatric surgeries and prolonged treatments.
  • Grassroots Diagnostics: Localized health camps and free pediatric check-ups.
  • Nutritional Support Networks: Fortified foods and supplements to combat malnutrition.
Educational Support

We contribute towards keeping children in school and ensuring wider academic access.

Financial hardship should never be the reason a bright mind leaves the classroom. Through Educational Support, we contribute towards reducing the hidden costs of schooling so that marginalized youth can focus on learning with dignity.

Our Focus Areas
  • Tuition Fee Assistance: Direct scholarship grants to prevent dropouts.
  • Essential Material Distribution: Uniforms, books, bags and stationery kits.
  • Higher Education Continuity: Merit-cum-means support for higher studies.
Empowerment Initiative

We contribute towards individual self-reliance and thriving micro-economies.

True empowerment comes from giving individuals the starting point they need to support themselves. Through our Empowerment initiative, we contribute towards building the capacity of women and unemployed youth so they can build sustainable livelihoods.

Our Focus Areas
  • Vocational Skill Training: Practical training in digital literacy and related sectors.
  • Early-Stage Guidance: Mentorship for bookkeeping, sales and business growth.
  • Seed Capital Ecosystems: Micro-grants for raw materials and machinery.

Eligibility / Selection Process

A transparent and accountable process ensuring assistance reaches those most in need.

Core Eligibility

To be eligible for assistance under our Healthcare, Education, or Livelihood programs, applicants must generally meet the following criteria:

Economic Status

Preference is given to individuals holding a valid BPL ration card, EWS certificate, or those with an annual family income below ₹2.5 Lakhs.

Documentation

Applicants must provide Aadhaar Card and proof of residency.

Non-Discrimination

Selection is made regardless of caste, creed, religion, or gender.

Program-Specific Criteria

  • Priority for life-threatening conditions and emergency surgeries.
  • Medical prescriptions, hospital estimates and diagnostic reports required.
  • Income Certificate required.
  • Preference for applicants not adequately covered under insurance or Ayushman Bharat.

  • Academic record and commitment to education assessed.
  • Admission Letter / Fee Structure required.
  • Income Certificate required.
  • Bonafide Certificate required.
  • Preference for first-generation learners, orphaned children and single-parent households.

  • Basic skill readiness or willingness to undergo vocational training.
  • Assessment of business viability.
  • Priority for women-led households and unemployed youth.
